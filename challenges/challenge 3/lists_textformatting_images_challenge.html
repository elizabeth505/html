How To Make A Really Good Chicken Sandwhich

Ingrediants:

  • Ciabatta Bread
  • Roman Lettuce
  • Garlic Butter
  • Tomatoe-basted Chicken
  • Baby Tomatoes
  • Organic Mayonnaise
  • (optional) Ground Black Pepper

Method:

  1. Place ciabatta bread into toaster for 1 minute on medium.
  2. Wash and slice approx. 6 baby tomatoes lengthwise.
  3. Microwave chicken for 2 minutes on medium.
  4. Slice chicken into slender lengths.
  5. Place ciabatta halves soft-side up on a plate.
  6. Wash 3 slices on lettuce, shake to dry.
  7. Spread approx. 2 teaspoons of mayo onto every second slice of bread.
  8. Spread approx. 1 teaspoon of garlic butter onto every empty slice of bread.
  9. Layer lettuce, tomatoes, and chicken onto butter slices.
  10. (Optional)Relish with ground black pepper.
